## 2025-05-14 - Keyboard Accessibility in Utility Apps
**Learning:** For utility applications like calculators, global keyboard support is a critical micro-UX improvement that bridges the gap between digital and physical interactions. Mapping the 'Backspace' key to delete and 'Escape' to clear provides a natural and expected experience for power users.
**Action:** When working on tool-like interfaces, always consider how physical keyboard mappings can enhance efficiency and accessibility.
