## 2026-07-19 - Accessible Calculator Enhancements
**Learning:** Adding descriptive ARIA labels to operator-only buttons and live regions to dynamic displays ensures an inclusive screen-reader experience. Furthermore, high-contrast `:focus-visible` styles with small transitions provide a seamless and satisfying experience for keyboard navigation users.
**Action:** Always verify keyboard accessibility of all interactive controls with `:focus-visible` and include polite live regions for interactive outputs.
