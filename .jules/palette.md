## 2026-07-18 - [Title] Keyboard Focus Indicators and Descriptive Aria Labels
**Learning:** Screen readers and keyboard users require clear visual focus indicators and descriptive ARIA labels to effectively navigate and use simple interactive apps like a calculator.
**Action:** Always provide descriptive `aria-label` attributes for symbol-based controls (like `Backspace`, `Clear`, `Multiply`) and style custom, high-contrast `:focus-visible` outlines to ensure seamless accessibility.
