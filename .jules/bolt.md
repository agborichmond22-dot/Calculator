**Learning:** When implementing a performance-optimized bypass of standard interpreter/parser routines like `eval()` with regular expressions, the patterns must be strictly designed to ensure they don't capture incomplete or empty operands. The regex must require at least one digit (e.g., using `(?:\d+(?:\.\d*)?|\.\d+)`) for robust validation and avoiding functional regressions.
